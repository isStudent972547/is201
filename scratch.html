I had the opportunity to serve as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in Newport Beach, California. I served for 18 months and had many opportunities to learn and to grow.

  1. Led and trained 20+ individuals weekly in one-on-one training meetings
  2. Connected with 100+ people weekly teaching 7 key principles for better living
  3. Acted as regional representative in over 25 meetings helping to optimize key performance indicators
  4. Managed the English Connect Language Learning Program Social Media Channels
    • Conducted bi-monthly meetings with regional representatives to analyze ad efficacy and define upcoming objectives
    • Created and executed content strategy posting 4x weekly to drive engagement and consistently reach KPIs
    • Published social media content tailored to the local region to promote participation in English Connect

I am grateful everyday for the lessons that I learned while serving a mission and also for every blessing that I was able to see come into my life because of it.

A mission is something that is optional for members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints but it is an amazing opportunity to learn and grow.
